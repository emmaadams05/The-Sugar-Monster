WHO ARE WE?

The Sugar Monster is a small local business found in New Haven, Connecticut. Our main goal is to bake delicious cookies made with natural ingredients to feed the customers of the city with the aid of local farms in New Haven County!

The Sugar Monster was founded in January of 2023 by its owners, Emma and Nivrith.
